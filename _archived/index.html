ReportWriter

Welcome to ReportWriter!

To keep things simple and secure, ReportWriter doesn't save any of your or your students' data, so there are no accounts or logins required.

Instead, to get started simply download the Excel template, add your students' data, save and then uplaod your saved template. Your reports will then open in the ReportWriter editor, where you can setup your comment bank and write your reports!

When you are finished writing your reports, simply press 'Save reports' to download an updated version of the Excel template which you can re-upload in future to carry on where you left off.
